About Us

Isn’t it utterly frustrating to find no vacant seats in a room? SeatMe is an app designed with students and customers in mind. SeatMe gives you the ability to reserve multiple seats before arriving at the location. A SeatMe compatible university or restaurant is designed to keep track on the number of present visitors in the room, as well as the vacant seats left in the room.

Each seat is attached with a thermal sensor to detect the presence of a human. When a person sits, the thermal sensor updates the information on the whole grid of seats, thereby updating the app’s contents.

Sensors are placed at every entrance of a building and their floors to keep track of the number of students in the room currently. In the event of a fire or emergency, the sensors trigger a buzzer until it records no persons in the room. The sensors also decide to switch off all lights when no person is present in the room, thereby conserving energy.
